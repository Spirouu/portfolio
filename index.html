My favorites projects

Here you will find three projects I have worked on. I would be delighted to discuss these projects with you!

Syncope Detector

I created a syncope detector based on data collected during the ascent of a spearfishing diver. An AI was tasked with calculating the probability of the diver experiencing syncope during their ascent and directly alerting the safety diver.

Lancet-free blood glucose monitor

During a university group project, I worked on a blood glucose sensor based on data collected using an infrared sensor on the patient's fingertip. Using a formula, we were able to determine the person's blood glucose level.

Cybersecurity intervention

I created presentations on cybersecurity awareness with a presentation module, Q&A, and a practical module with hacking exercises.
